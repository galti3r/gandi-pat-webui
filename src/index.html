Gandi DNS Manager

Manage your DNS records using the Gandi LiveDNS API. Enter your Personal Access Token to get started. Your token needs "Manage domain technical configurations" permission.

Token Storage

Your token is stored locally in your browser and sent directly to the Gandi API. It is never transmitted to any third-party server.
